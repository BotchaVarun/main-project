Get To Know About

Welcome to Grandhalayam, a universal open-source platform for seamless book and magazine management. Our mission is to create an accessible digital library where users can effortlessly borrow and return books, explore diverse categories, and stay updated with the latest magazines. Grandhalayam thrives on community contributions, allowing anyone to add and share their favorite reads. Join us to be part of a global community passionate about the free exchange of knowledge and the love of reading.
